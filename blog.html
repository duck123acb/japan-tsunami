The Events of The Tsunami of 2011

Going to Spain:
This is one of the more interesting stories. I went to the King Fernando the VII (7th) of Spain, but he was all like: “nO, wE haVe cHrIsToPHeR CoLuMbuS.” Or something like that, so I went all the way to England, but they were like (in their British posh voice): “oOoOoH tHe KiNg WoNt LiKe YoU, wItH yOuR sTuPiD iTaLiAn NaMe” So I went and changed it to my English name: “John Cabot”. I went back in, and the guards treated me as if I was an entirely new person. Anyway, I went in, and saw him, King Henry the VII (7th). After being refused once before, I was kinda nervous, but I got the job, and left with high spirits.

Second adventure:
After my first attempt at sailing, we had no luck. But on my second journey, that's where it gets interesting. We found land! “ASIA!!!!!” my crew screamed! Little did they know... “No, good sirs, this is new, undiscovered land! I'm naming it New Found Land.” I replied, feeling excited. “Let's look for treasure!” one said, excited. “Not with that cod you won't” another said. “OMGZZZ CODD!!!” I screamed, very unexplainably, “Let's get some!” My crew lowered a bucket, and the bucket was filled with cod! I very professionally resisted the urge to sing “Here some cod, there some cod, everywhere some cod cod OLD MC CABOT FOUND NEW LAND, EE III EEE III *pause for dramatic effect* OOOOOOOOOO!” Anyway, that is the end of this post! P.S. The king loved my discovery of cod. *Annoyed crew member who told me where the cod was starts to get angry*

This might be the last post...
This blog post is from under the deck. It has been a terrible storm, and we are just hanging on. We might not make it to land, and I miss my wife and children, especially Sebastian. There is shouting in the background. Like I said, this maybe my last post. I must go. My crew needs me. Bye...

We're Safe!
We survived! We managed to dock at Ireland, and now we are back at sea! For a moment, I thought we were going to die. Now, there are no storm clouds in any direction. *Looks out window* Oh no... There are some storm clouds on the horizon, but it's very faint. But the ship is going fast. We will hit it soon, so I better warn my crew.

This might be the last post... again
We're in a storm again, and this time a wimp just left on a small boat. “I wAnna LIvE” he screamed as he left. Like I said, wimp. I started thinking of achieving immortality, as I want to continue exploring. The astrolabe and compass are not working. I think... we are lost at sea. This is it for today. If you see all these posts, then it means that I have achieved immortality. Bye... For the last time...
