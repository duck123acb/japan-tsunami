The Events of The Tsunami of 2011

The Day Before
Date: Mar. 10th, 2011
Everything was fine, just another day in the middle-eastern part of Japan. Everyone was having a regular day, but no one was prepared for what would happen next...

The Tsunami
Date: Mar. 11th, 2011
People woke up, probably thinking: "Oh, it's another day of work, then another night of rest", but little did they know... at around 2:46 p.m. (JST, of course)
the tsunami was spotted, and then a few moments later it came CRASHING down. It mosty affected in the middle of Honshu on the east side, but in a way (economy, familly, etc.) it affected all of Japan, and even some of the rest of the world.
